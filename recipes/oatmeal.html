Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is an oatmeal-based, oatmeal-like mush made of cooked oats.

You might like it.

If you don't like it don't force yourself to eat it. Find something else to eat.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Water
  • Whatever else you want in your oatmeal

Steps

  1. Put water in a pot
  2. Put oats in the water in the pot
  3. Heat up the water in the pot (this also heats up the oats)
  4. Stop heating up the water when you feel like you want to eat the oats
  5. Wait a minute
  6. Eat the oats
  7. At some point in the process outlined above add whatever else you want to put in the oatmeal into the pot with the water and the oats
    • I am not you, nor am I your mother. I cannot tell you what you might like to eat in your oatmeal. Take some responsibility.
