Glass of Water

Description

Since the dawn of time water-drinking has been thirst's dire enemy.

If you have the time to do so put your water in a glass before you drink it.

Water-drinking is necessary to live.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Glass

Steps

  1. Put water in a glass
    • As noted above, do this only if you have time
  2. Drink the water out of the glass (or in whatever manner makes sense if you don't have time to use a glass)
